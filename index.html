Our Story

A Kitchen Built
on Passion

FreshBites started as a small family kitchen in the heart of Chennai in 2001. What began as a dream to serve honest, wholesome food has grown into a beloved café experience — without ever compromising on taste or quality.


We source our vegetables fresh each morning, grind our own spices, and bake our breads in-house. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is our reputation on a dish.
